COVID-19Tracker

See it here

This web-app allows users to track the spread of the Corona virus through different graphs. It gives the option of seeing graphs of the whole world or a specific country. Users can also choose a specific date and a stepper function to see cases against deaths.

This was made by gathering data from an API that provides up to date numbers on the virus as well as data collected since the outbreak began. I also made use of Chart.js to allow a visual representation of the data being returned.

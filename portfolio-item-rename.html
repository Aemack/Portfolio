Remember TheWords

See it here

This web-app was designed to help users memorize the words to poems, lyrics, speeches or anything else split by lines. It does this by going through each line, showing it to you and then asking you to either type it or use your microphone to dictate.

Most of the program revolves around manipulating strings to find lines and ignore certain characters. The voice-recognition is achieved by using the Speech Recognition API. Reciting the line out loud should help in remembering especially when asked for multiple lines at a time.
